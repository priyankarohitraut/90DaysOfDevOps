* Process management
ps: display a current process 
top: real time of running processes 
htop: an interactive, more user friendly alternative to top
kill: sends a termination signal to a specific process ID.
pkill: searches for and kills processes based on their name or other attributes.
pidof: return the process ID of a running program.
pgrep: search for processes by name and list their PIDs.
nice / renice: starts a process the priority of an already running process.
jobs / bg / fg: list background jobs,resumes suspended jobs in the background,or bring 
                 them to the foreground.
 ps aux : display a detailed  list of all the running  program on the system .
 systemctl: manage the system service and the process into the your system.
